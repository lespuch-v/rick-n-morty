Rick and Morty

Rick and Morty is a show created by Justin Roiland and Dan Harmon, and it's based on the characters Dr. Emmett Brown and Marty McFly in Back to the Future (1985). Rick is an alcoholic sociopath and scientist. Morty is a dimwit grandson who takes on absurd adventures.
